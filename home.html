Liquidity

December 19, 2022

Price seeks liquidity in the form of HIGHS, LOWS and GAPS

  • Buy Stops rest above HIGHS
  • Sell Stops rest below LOWS

Price runs TO and THROUGH

  • Monthly HIGHS and LOWS
  • Weekly HIGHS and LOWS
  • Daily HIGHS and LOWS
  • Session HIGHS and LOWS

If price does not close above the previous HIGH or below the previous LOW, the state of price delivery changes (price will seek liquidity in the opposite direction).

Fair Value Gap

December 19, 2022

A Fair Value Gap forms when the current candle does not share price with the one previous to the last.

  • High[2] < Low[0]
  • Low[2]  > High[0]

  • Index_2 forms a LOW followed by a HIGHER LOW

Bearish:

  • Enter short position on Index_1

FVG Entry Checklist

December 19, 2022

Bullish:

  • Stop Hunt of previous LOW or relative equal LOWS (1h and 15m)
  • Wait for Market Structure Shift (5m, 2m, 1m)
  • Identify Discount FVG with MSS within displacement range (5m, 2m, 1m)
  • Enter long position on retrace to FVG around OTE Fib levels
  • Exit at previous 1h / 15m swing HIGH or GAP
Bearish:
  • Stop Hunt of previous HIGH or relative equal HIGHS (1h and 15m)
  • Wait for Market Structure Shift (5m, 2m, 1m)
  • Identify Premium FVG with MSS within displacement range (5m, 2m, 1m)
  • Enter short position on retrace to FVG around OTE Fib levels
  • Exit at previous 1h / 15m swing LOW or GAP

Breaker Entry Checklist

December 19, 2022

Bullish:

  • Stop hunt takes out previous LOW
  • Price breaks above previous swing HIGH
  • Previous swing HIGH is the BREAKER (most recent bullish candle body)
  • Enter on retrace to BREAKER level
Bearish:
  • Stop hunt takes out previous HIGH
  • Price breaks below previous swing LOW
  • Previous swing LOW is the BREAKER (most recent bearish candle body)
  • Enter on retrace to BREAKER level
