Liquidity

Price seeks liquidity in the form of HIGHS, LOWS and GAPS

  • Buy Stops rest above HIGHS
  • Sell Stops rest below LOWS

Price runs To and Through

  • Monthly HIGHS and LOWS
  • Weekly HIGHS and LOWS
  • Daily HIGHS and LOWS
  • Session HIGHS and LOWS

If price does not close above previous HIGHS or below previous LOWS, the state of price delivery changes (price reverses).

Fair Value Gaps

A Fair Value Gap forms when candle 1 and candle 3 do not have any overlap in price.
The highlighted displacement range on candle 2 is the gap or price inefficency.

Bullish FVG Entry Checklist

STEP 01
Stop Hunt of previous LOW or relative equal LOWS (1h and 15m)

STEP 02
Wait for Market Structure Shift (5m, 2m, 1m)

STEP 03
Wait for FVG to form when previous short term HIGH is taken out (5m, 2m, 1m)

STEP 04
Enter on retrace to FVG at one of the OTE Fib levels below equilibrium

STEP 05
Take profit at previous swing HIGHS or GAPS

STEP 02

STEP 04
